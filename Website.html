Hong Kong

(Flag of Hong Kong)
Hong Kong, officially the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic of China is a metropolitan area and special administrative region of the People's Republic of China on the eastern Pearl River Delta of the South China Sea. With over 7.5 million people of various nationalities in a 1,104-square-kilometre (426 sq mi) territory, Hong Kong is one of the most densely populated places in the world.

Growing up in Hong Kong wasn't a typical Movie-esc lifestyle, for one, we don't typically drive cars in Hong Kong, we take ferrys between the many islands that make up Hong kong just to get to work or school everyday. I go to an International school, which is very interesting, as I am attuned to everybody's culture and personality, and very aware to all sorts of cultures from around the world. We also learn about all the issues in the world, with no bias towards our own country, for example, local issues or government.

In my opinion, Hong kong is probably one of the best cities in the world when it comes to quality of life, with a lot of factors a lot of other cities or countries dont have, for example, Hong Kong is a very diverse place to live, learning about local and other cultures from all the way across the world. Hong Kong also has great public transport, it is a very small place, and a lot of moving around in Hong Kong is on foot, or by its train system "MTR", the MTR system is capable of taking you across the city in a matter of minutes.
